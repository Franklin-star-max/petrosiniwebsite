Frequently Asked Questions

When will Asaba Modern Mall open?

We're excited to announce that Asaba Modern Mall is scheduled to open soon. We're currently in the final stages of preparation and will announce the official opening date shortly.

The mall will feature our three main departments: Home Appliances, Lands & Real Estate, and Generators, along with several other retail sections for a complete shopping experience.

Yes, we offer delivery services within Asaba and surrounding areas. Delivery fees vary based on location and product size. Contact us for specific delivery information.

We accept various payment methods including cash, bank transfers, and card payments. We're committed to making your shopping experience as convenient as possible.
